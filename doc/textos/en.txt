The degree of collective pessimism gripping us is more desperate and immobilizer than financial imponderables. CWC, a motorcycle trip through the countries starring the *CRISIS* (PIGS) revealing people and projects which gave an optimistic and innovative answer to it.


**BACKGROUND**

In October 2012 it was planned to start this trip, but 2 weeks before 
that someone stole from the office all the audiovisual equipment, which halted the project. In
February 2013 Helena was born, which delayed the trip up to this moment.

This documentary is also a gift for Helena, and all this generation, so that when
she can watch it with analysis capabilities she spot the world and the context in which
she was born, hoping that in some manner it can help her facing her life.


**WHAT?**

CWC is a documentary portraying the 2014's Europe. A solo motorcycle
trip from Galicia, in the Iberian Peninsula's Northwest extreme,
to the Pireo in Greece, across Portugal, Spain and Italy (PIGS)*.

It's a critical eye to the CRISIS. A practical and brave answer to that questions
we should ask:

**What is the crisis?

Where does it come from?

Where does it lead us?

And now what?**


**WHY?**

The crisis that was a deceleration and now seems like an hecatomb had to last two
years and it'll make six. We don't see the light at the end of the tunnel, and if we saw it
we would think that it's the train coming to finish us. We have fear, and as british sociologist Frank Furedi said long before the crisis arose, "ubiquitous and persistent fear generates doom and resignation".

We need to visualize alternative answers, other possible futures, because if we don't
imagine them, we will never be able to make them happen.


**WHO?**

My name is Xavier Belho and I'm devoted to graphical design and audiovisual
production. If I can - with your help - start this project I'll make a trip
in a motorcycle, a thirdhand Yamaha Virago 1100 of 1997, to shoot a
documentary that will take me from the galician Fisterra to the ____, through
the countries starring the CRISIS (PIGS).

**CÓMO?**

Viajando en moto no puedo permitirme llevar un voluminoso equipo: contaré
con una pequeña cámara de vídeo y fotos que grabará en fullHD, un miniequipo de
sonido, un portátil para ir editando algunas imágenes y subirlas a la red a medida que
voy avanzando.

El proyecto se presenta como una construcción modular con dos etapas
diferenciadas:

1. Los protagonistas contarán su historia, allí donda la desarrollan,
directamente a la cámara, a través de pequeñas entrevistas tipo videomatón. Estas
microentrevistas se irán subiendo a la web y a las redes sociales, creando así un mapa
interactivo del viaje con el que el público podrá interactuar, prácticamente, en directo.

2. Una vez terminado el viaje, se montará un documental narrativo en el que se
complementarán declaraciones seleccionadas de los protagonistas con información e
imágenes del contexto en el que desarrollan su actividad.

El material del documental tendrá una licencia Creative Commons, de forma que
se puedan utilizar las imágenes para desarrollar otros proyectos, deseando
que, quien apueste por este documental, se involucre, participe, y lo haga
suyo ayudándonos a darle difusión.

El documental se presentará a los festivales de documentales internacionales y
nacionales.

**A DÓNDE IRÁN VUESTRAS APORTACIONES**

El proyecto no se puede realizar sin vuestra ayuda. Con esta campaña espero
